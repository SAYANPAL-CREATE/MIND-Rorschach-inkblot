Merged Mental Health Prototype

Choose your assessment pathway.

This website combines two separate pipelines. The first uses a structured mental-health questionnaire. The second uses the Rorschach inkblot workflow with rule-based NLP interpretation.

Important disclaimer: This is an educational and experimental digital-health prototype. It does not provide a clinical diagnosis and cannot replace a licensed mental-health professional.

Pipeline 1: Questionnaire

Structured questions with patient-friendly categories for depression, anxiety, and stress.


Pipeline 2: Rorschach Inkblot

Ten-plate response collection with transparent rule-based NLP indicators.
